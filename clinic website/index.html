Our Medical Services

General Surgery

Offers a wide range of surgical procedures for conditions like appendicitis, hernias, and more, ensuring precise care and post-operative support

Paediatric Service

Provides specialized healthcare for children, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment of illnesses.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Comprehensive care for women's health, including pregnancy, childbirth, and gynecological conditions.

Radio Diagnostic / Ultrasound Services

Advanced imaging technology for accurate diagnosis of internal conditions and prenatal monitoring.

ECG / Stress ECG Services

Non-invasive tests to monitor heart function and detect abnormalities during rest and physical exertion.

Laboratory Services

Dental & Eye Clinic Services

Medicine & Pharmacy

Aviation Medical Service

Specialized health assessments and certifications for pilots and aircrew, ensuring they meet the required physical and mental standards for aviation safety.
